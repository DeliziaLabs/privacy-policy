Privacy Policy for Delizia

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Introduction

Delizia ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the App:

  • Account Information: Name, username, email address, password, date of birth, and nationality
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, bio, dietary preferences (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, kosher, halal, dairy-free), food allergies and their severity, favorite dishes, and disliked dishes
  • Content You Create: Recipes (including titles, ingredients, instructions, cooking times, images), events (including names, dates, locations, guest lists, descriptions, images), shopping lists, and event photos/memories
  • Social Information: Friend connections, friend requests, recipe shares, and event invitations

Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Information: App features you use, recipes you view, events you create or attend
  • Log Data: Error logs and performance data to improve app functionality

Permissions We Request

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Camera Access: To take photos of recipes, events, profile pictures, and event memories
  • Photo Library Access: To select existing photos from your device for recipes, events, and profile pictures
  • Biometric Authentication (Face ID/Touch ID/Fingerprint): Optional - to provide secure and convenient login (data stored locally on your device only)
  • Notifications: To send you updates about friend requests, recipe shares, and event invitations

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable recipe sharing and event planning features
  • Facilitate social connections (friend requests, event invitations)
  • Generate personalized shopping lists based on your recipes and events
  • Send you notifications about app activity (with your permission)
  • Respond to your requests, questions, and feedback
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security threats
  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience
